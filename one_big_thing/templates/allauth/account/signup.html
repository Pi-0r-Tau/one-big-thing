Welcome to One Big Thing

One Big Thing is a learning and development opportunity for all civil servants. This year the theme is data. You'll need to complete this learning by December 2023.

For One Big Thing you'll need to:

  • complete a survey to find your learning level
  • do up to 7 hours of learning
  • record your learning
  • feedback on your experience of One Big Thing

By taking part in One Big Thing you'll improve your data skills and confidence, and be comfortable making better use of data in your role.
