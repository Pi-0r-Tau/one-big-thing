Welcome to the One Big Thing Learning Record

One Big Thing is a new annual initiative for civil servants to take shared action around a reform priority, as one Civil Service.

The ‘One Big Thing’ for 2023 is data - every civil servant will complete at least one day’s worth of data training this autumn.

The One Big Thing Learning Record is your space to record your learning.

When you sign in for the first time you will complete a survey to find out what learning is best for you.
