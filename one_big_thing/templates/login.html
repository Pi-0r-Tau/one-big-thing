Welcome to One Big Thing

One Big Thing is a learning and development opportunity for all civil servants. This year the aim is to improve your data skills and confidence. You'll need to complete this learning by December 2023.

For One Big Thing you'll need to:

  • complete a survey to find your learning level
  • do 7 hours of learning
  • record your learning
  • feedback on your experience of One Big Thing

Sign in
